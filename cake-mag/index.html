Cake Magazine
May 2024
Editorial, Typography

Complete concept for a magazine released monthly focused on cake. Pale colors and round typography emulate smooth buttercream frosting and allow saturated color photography to stand out. A 10 column 9 row grid allows for flexibility between spreads and issues and easily accommodates graphic elements, photography, and text.

A simple and round sans-serif provides legibility and friendliness to the body text while a slightly quirky serif provides textural accents in pull quotes and subheadings.
