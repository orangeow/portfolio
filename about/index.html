Olivia Weissflog is a graphic design student at Parsons School of Design in New York, NY. After graduating magna cum laude with a bachelors degree in political science and a minor in mathematics from UCLA in 2021, she worked as a legal assistant for a year before deciding to change course and go back to school to study design.

Experience with C++, R, and higher level mathematics has allowed her to quickly learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript and fall in love with the way graphic design exists at the intersection of art and math. She is currently seeking an internship or part-time design position for summer and fall 2024.
