My work draws on a wide range of references reflecting my varied academic and professional background. I face every project with an organized and methodical approach. My pieces are uniquely designed for their purposes while reflecting a consistent appreciation for color, pattern, and typography.

Learning how to code has given me an entirely new creative medium to explore that appeals strongly to my background in math and knowing what is possible with code allows me to more effectively design for the digital landscape.
